About Us

Mesh ’N It Up was created by Tiffany Canaday, a disabled single mom with a lifelong love for crafting and creating with her hands.

Living with Parkinson’s disease, Tiffany began making wreaths, candles, and handmade décor as a way to stay creative, active, and connected while working from home.

What started as a personal passion quickly became something bigger — a small, family-supported business rooted in creativity, resilience, and love. Tiffany is proudly assisted by her sister, niece, and daughter, who help bring each piece to life.

Every item from Mesh ’N It Up is made with care, patience, and heart. These creations aren’t just décor — they represent perseverance, family, and the joy of making something meaningful.

Thank you for supporting a small, family-run business and being part of our story.
