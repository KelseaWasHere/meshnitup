About Us

Mesh ’N It Up is a small, family-run craft business where creativity and care meet. Every item we make is handcrafted with love, and our mission is to bring a personal touch to your home and gifts.

Tiffany’s Story

Tiffany Canaday, a disabled single mom, started Mesh ’N It Up to channel her creativity into something meaningful. With Parkinson’s, she focuses on projects that are both therapeutic and fulfilling, making each piece with dedication and care.

Our Team

Tiffany is supported by her sister, niece, and daughter. Together, they ensure that every project is handcrafted to perfection, while sharing skills, stories, and joy in every creation.

Our Mission

We aim to create beautiful, personalized home decor and gifts while fostering creativity and independence. Every wreath, candle, or blanket is a unique expression of care and craftsmanship.
